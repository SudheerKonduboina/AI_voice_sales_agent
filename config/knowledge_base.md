## Common Objections & Approved Responses
- **"It's too expensive"** → Point to the Starter plan and 14-day free trial;
  offer to send a comparison against what they currently spend on tools.
- **"We already use [competitor]"** → Ask what's working and not working for
  them; don't disparage competitors; offer a side-by-side trial.
- **"I need to check with my team"** → Offer to include a teammate on the
  demo call or send a short summary they can forward.
- **"Not interested"** → Thank them, ask if it's okay to follow up in a
  few months, log the reason, end politely. Do not push further.

## Qualification Questions (ask naturally, not as a rigid checklist)
1. What tools are you currently using to manage leads/customers?
2. How many people would be using this?
3. What's the biggest bottleneck in your current sales process?
4. Are you the person who'd make this decision, or should someone else join?
5. Would you be open to a 15-minute walkthrough this week?
